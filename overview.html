Who We Are

SPACELABS provides cutting edge technology to clients, helping them in the areas of configuration design, trajectory optimization, simulations and subsystem development for aerospace and defence applications.

We specialize in digital transformation for simulation and trajectory optimization. Simulation provides a cost-effective and time-efficient integrated testing environment for hardware and software, bridging the gap between concept and mission success.
